Frequently Asked Questions

What does Throwing Shapes mean...?

Throwing Shapes is an Irish idiom for dancing to electronic music while gesticulating with ones arms and hands. Throwing shapes can also mean to act tough, in a fight for example, where a comabtant may use wild and flamboyant gestures to intimidate his or her apponent without having any real skill or ability. A Shaper might me a person who likes to impress other people with their style or flashy moves, often for comedic effect.

Where do your t-shirts come from?

Throwing Shapes is an Irish owned business. We design everything ourselves and we source everything, from our cotton tees to our printer Aine in Donegal, here in Ireland to try and keep Irish jobs going.

Where do you ship to?

We'll ship anywhere within reason. If you live outside of Ireland, just send us your details and we'll try and get our products to you. Shipping costs may vary.

How long will delivery take?

Once we have your order, we dispatch the next day. If you're in Ireland, you should receive your t-shirt in 3 to 5 working days. Outside of Ireland can take anything over 5 days, contact us with your location and we'll get that information for you

What payment methods do you accept?

Right now, we only accept PayPal, but we hope to take credit cards in the near future... watch this space!

I'm not happy with my order... what do I do?

We're the good guys, if you don't like your tee for any reason, just send it back to it in the condition in which it was received and we'll refund you right away

My order hasn't arrived! What do I do?

There are a hundred different reasons your package might not arrive. But don't worry, contact us with the details of your order and we'll do our best to put things right.
